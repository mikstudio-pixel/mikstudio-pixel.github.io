ThisChairDoesNotExist

Valeriya Truskavetska, Marťa Hrnčiříková, Vít Olšina, Jiří Šprtel, Tristan Tvrdý, Jakub Hrdina

Tomas Bata University in Zlín

The latest iteration of ModificAI explores the possibilities of generating chair designs using text prompts and reverse processes. By intersecting digital and traditional design, it brings new creative approaches using 3D printing.

From End to End

various authors

Bezalel Academy of Arts and Design Jerusalem

Inspired by the oldest leather shoe by Ötzi, the authors' original reimagination, combining craft with technology, have given it a new interpretation reflecting today's society and environment.

VISTA

Hana Kobierská

Tomas Bata University in Zlín

The collection of silver jewelry is inspired by tree rings, symbolizing the slow and patient process of growth. They remind us that in nature, as in everyday life, it is important to give things time to reach their full potential.

Stools Shuttlecock

Benjamín Korbela

Technical University in Zvolen

A pair of seating elements articulates the inspiration of badminton. It presents a look into the background of this pairs sport and transforms its characteristic features into an authentic form of furniture.

Light and Darkness

Bianka Demčáková

Tomas Bata University in Zlín

A short animated film about the power of social media to draw us into a world that is difficult to escape from. It follows the main character as he becomes entangled in the virtual world.

Bamboo Whispers

Leong Ka Wai

National Taipei University of Technology

Inspired by bamboo, this coffee set blends Eastern cultural elements with modern minimalism. It includes a dripper, cup, and pour-over kettle—each usable individually or combined.

DepoRooms

Hana Šegedová

Technical University in Zvolen

The project transforms an old water tower into short-term accommodation, where less is more. The compact cells provide everything necessary and, thanks to thoughtful solutions, they do not need anything extra.
